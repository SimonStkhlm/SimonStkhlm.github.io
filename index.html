About

Hejhej! I am Simon Sahli. IT Consultant, Project- and Application Manager and Business Processing Specialist.

I am looking for a new job opportunity in Stockholm and I would like to give a quick overview of my professional life, my person and what I am exactly looking for in a job.


My General Objective

To implement well-chosen and intelligently configured software with the needed, to provide the necessary knowledge to users and to optimise processes for users and entire companies.


Work overview

I have 10 years of experience in various industries and positions within the IT sector. I started with 1st level hardware and software support in a public administration organisation, where I also completed my IT apprenticeship. After that, I went to a bank where, in addition to hardware and software support, I was also more involved in projects and was responsible for certain applications. And my last job, where I worked for 5 1/2 years, I was as a Consultant for a software company. The first half I was in the Support team with smaller Cloud projects and the second half I got promoted to the Project team. There I was responsible for several customers and I managed and executed several software implementation projects as well.


Desired Job Profile

In the last few years I have realised that I really enjoy working on projects, especially because I like to build something and be part of the whole process from requirements gathering to conception over realisation and finally to going live. So I am looking for an IT position where I can be part of interesting projects and I can be part of them over the entire period. I can take over parts of the projects at the beginning and after adequate knowledge has been built up, also the entire project. Agile projects are of particular interest to me, but I am open to all kind of projects. However, I am also not averse to support, as you can learn a lot doing support work and users can of course continue to expect my support after a project has gone live. Giving trainings and writing documentation/concepts are also welcome to be part of my job profile. From the technical side, I am open and always interested in getting to know new technologies. A big plus to work with would be the Atlassian products, especially Jira and Confluence.


Personal description

I'm a positive person with many interests. One big interest is science and the technologies that have emerged from it. Especially information and communication technology, of course, but also fundamental findings in physics interest me. In computer science, I find it especially interesting how the different elements/technologies interact with each other. My strength is that I can distinguish the different elements well and localise problems or approaches to solutions (troubleshooting). I proceed in a structured and analysed way (buttom-up or top-down).

In my opinion, communication is a key element in IT. In my work, I therefore place importance on communicating precisely and as intensively as necessary with the stakeholders involved. I think I can respond well to the needs of the different stakeholders and find solutions together with them.
