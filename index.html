About Me

Hej hej! I am Simon Sahli, Software Consultant, Project Manager and Business Data Processing Specialist.

I am looking for a new job opportunity in Stockholm. With this website I want to give you an overview of my career, about myself and what kind of job I am looking for.


My General Objective

To implement well-chosen and intelligently configured software, to provide the necessary knowledge to users and to optimise processes for entire companies.


Work Overview

I have 10 years of experience in various industries and positions within the IT sector. I started with 1st level hardware and software support in a public administration organisation, where I also completed my IT apprenticeship. Afterwards, I joined the IT team of a bank, where I was involved in projects and responsible for certain applications (ERP banking system and VoIP). Finally, I have worked for 6 1/2 years as a Consultant for a software manufacturer company in the FinTech industry. I was first involved in the Support team with Cloud projects and later got promoted to the Project team, where I was responsible for several customers, managing and executing software implementation projects.


Desired Job Profile

During the last few years, I have realised that I really enjoy working on projects, especially because I like to build something and be part of the whole process from the requirements gathering to conception over realisation and finally to going live. So, I am looking for an IT position where I could be part of interesting projects. I could take over parts of projects at first, and after building up adequate knowledge, lead entire projects as well. Agile projects are of particular interest to me. However, I find support work rewarding too, because you can help clients and learn more about the product at the same time. I also find essential that users can count on my support after a project has gone live. Giving training and writing documentation/concepts work is also welcome to be part of my job. From the technical side, I am open and always interested in getting to know new technologies. I consider it a plus to work with Atlassian products, especially Jira and Confluence.


Personal Description

I am a positive person with many interests, one of them being science and the technologies that have emerged from it. Not only information and communication advances, but also fundamental findings in physics fascinate me. As for computer science, I find especially intriguing how different systems interact with each other. My strength is that I can distinguish the different elements well, localise problems (troubleshooting) and find solutions. To achieve that, I proceed in a structured and analysed way (bottom-up or top-down).

In my opinion, communication is a key element in IT. I therefore place importance on communicating precisely and as intensively as necessary. In practice, I am able to accomodate the needs of different stakeholders and to find solutions together with them.
